# Location Image Generation Plan

This plan outlines the visual requirements for the newly added Contemporary and Future locations.

## 1. Contemporary Era - Missing Images

| Location | Era | Visual Prompts / Keywords | Mood |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cheonggyecheon (1950s)** | 1950s | Shanty town along the stream, wooden shacks, laundry hanging, people washing clothes, sepia tone or desaturated colors. | Post-war, Poor, Gritty |
| **Busan Provisional Capital** | 1950s | Busy street with refugees, government buildings with sandbags, trams, military jeeps, chaotic but lively. | Urgent, Crowded, Historical |
| **Gukje Market** | 1950s | Market stalls with US military goods, refugees selling items, narrow alleys, steam from food stalls. | Bustling, Survival, Warm |
| **Pyeonghwa Market** | 1970s | Brick building facade with many small windows, piles of fabric, sewing machines visible, neon signs in retro font. | Industrial, Busy, Retro |


| **Gwanghwamun (1987)** | 1980s | Tear gas fog, protestors with banners, riot police lines, cathedral or palace in distance, dramatic lighting. | Tense, Revolutionary, Smoky |
| **Seoul Olympic Stadium** | 1988 | Jamsil Stadium with Olympic rings, colorful flags, doves flying, bright blue sky, festive atmosphere. | Celebratory, Grand, Historic |
| **Gangnam Teheran-ro** | 1990s/2000s | Skyscrapers under construction or newly built, wide avenue with traffic, "Venture Valley" feel, sunset reflecting on glass. | Modern, Urban, Rising |
| **DDP (Dongdaemun Design Plaza)** | 2010s | Futuristic silver curved architecture, night view with LED lights, fashion people, fluid shapes. | Artistic, Modern, Sleek |
| **Incheon Int'l Airport** | 2000s | Glass architecture, airplanes visible through window, airy and spacious terminal, travelers with suitcases. | Global, Clean, Open |

## Technical Specs
- **Style**: Semi-realistic digital painting / Concept art style to match existing assets.
- **Ratio**: 16:9 for backgrounds (Location), 1:1 for thumbnails.
